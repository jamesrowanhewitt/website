Why this guide exists

What is a living style guide

In programming the documentation process is often the weakest strength that programmers have, yet its one of the most important steps in programming.

Living style guides are here to help us transform our code into well-described libraries. This maintains consistency and enchances the development process of a website.

Living style guides have the word living for a reason, they are dynamic and grow with the development process.

Why is a living style guide useful to developers and designers

A living style guide makes the development process easier, as its a central resource for all the colour palletes, types of headings, button styles etc.

As such a living style guide prevents alot of confusion or fustration in trying to keep a consistent website, especially in large teams where the design process can often get out of control.

My list of living style guide tips

  1. Don't be pedantic, allow for some slack
  2. Doesn't have to be perfect, we just need a guide to have a consistent website
  3. Essential elements: overview, rational for work, information about colour palette and logos.
  4. Formulate a easy to read guide, preferbly with infographics or photos.
