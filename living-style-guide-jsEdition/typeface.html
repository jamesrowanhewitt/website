Typography

Font styles, forms and buttons

Fonts

font: 15px/1.5 Arial, Helvetica,sans-serif;

Fonts chosen

  • Arial
  • Helvetica
  • Sans-Serif

Note: We have chosen these 3 fonts as they are compatible on all browsers which helps with accessiblity.

Buttons

Buttons must follow the criteria:

  1. Highlight when mouse hovers over to aid accessiblity
  2. Stay highlighted when on the selected page - this is important to help with navigation and accessibility
